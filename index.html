What We Do

A studio built around six disciplines, run as one practice.

From first sketch to final handover, every discipline sits under one roof — so the architecture and the interior are never designed twice.

Re

Residential

Private homes and apartments designed around how a family actually moves through a day.

Ho

Hospitality

Hotels, clubs and restaurants where atmosphere is engineered, not decorated.

Wo

Workplace

Offices and corporate headquarters built for focus, culture and quiet productivity.

In

Interiors

Furniture, material and lighting design carried through with the same hand as the architecture.

La

Landscape

Gardens and outdoor rooms treated as the fifth elevation of every project.

Ad

Adaptive Reuse

Heritage and existing structures restored and reimagined for a modern life.

How We Work

Four stages, one point of contact throughout.

No project moves to the next stage until you've signed off on the one before it.

01

Discovery

Site visit, brief, budget and timeline — we listen before we draw a single line.
02

Concept & Design

Spatial planning, material direction and 3D visualisation, refined together with you.
03

Documentation

Working drawings, structural coordination and vendor tendering, ready for site.
04

Execution & Handover

On-site supervision through to styling, snagging and final handover of the keys.

The Studio

Founded on one idea: design for a better quality of life.

Space Enviro Architects is an independent architecture and interior design practice. Every project is led personally by our principal architect, from the first site visit to the day the keys are handed over.

We work in small numbers by choice — a limited slate of projects each year, so that no client is ever a name on a list.
